Measure premium income against capped upside.

Enter a ticker, pick the history window, then test a serial covered-call program using your chosen expiration cadence and out-of-the-money percentage. If you provide implied volatility, the page estimates premium with Black-Scholes and compares total strategy P/L to simply holding the stock.
